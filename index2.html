Rock-Paper-Scissors

A Rock-Paper-Scissors game built using HTML, CSS, and Vanilla Javascript. When the Player choose one of the options from the left, the comp will choose a random option. If Player lose the round, Player's points will get subtracted. Whoever reach ten points first, will be the winner.

A React app that search through HackerRank news based on the inputted keyword. It uses Redux & React-Redux as container and to manage the app state & Redux-Saga for any asynchronous action (which is fetching data)
